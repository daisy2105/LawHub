LABOUR AND INDUSTRIAL LAW – KEY POINTS

• Regulates relationship between employers, employees, and trade unions.
• Ensures fair wages, safe working conditions, and protection against exploitation.
• Important Acts: Industrial Disputes Act, 1947; Factories Act, 1948; Minimum Wages Act, 1948; Code on Wages, 2019.
• Constitutional provisions:
  – Article 23: Prohibition of forced labour.
  – Article 24: Prohibition of child labour.
  – Article 39, 41–43A: Right to work, fair wages, and participation of workers.
• Promotes social justice and industrial peace.
