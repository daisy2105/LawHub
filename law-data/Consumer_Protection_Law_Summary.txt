CONSUMER PROTECTION LAW – KEY POINTS

• Aims to safeguard consumers from unfair trade practices and defective goods/services.
• Consumer Protection Act, 2019 – major law ensuring consumer rights.
• Recognized rights: Right to safety, information, choice, redressal, education.
• Establishes three-tier redressal system: District, State, and National Commissions.
• Promotes accountability in business and protects consumers from exploitation.
• Linked to Article 21 (Right to life), Article 38 (Social justice), Article 47 (Public health).
• Encourages ethical trade and fair market practices.
