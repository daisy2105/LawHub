CIVIL LAW – KEY POINTS

• Civil law deals with private rights and liabilities between individuals or organizations.
• Covers disputes over property, contracts, family relations, and torts.
• Major branches: Contract law, Property law, Family law, and Law of Torts.
• Remedies include damages, injunctions, and specific performance.
• Civil Procedure Code (CPC), 1908 – governs procedures for civil cases.
• Burden of proof: "Preponderance of probabilities" (less strict than criminal cases).
• Constitutional basis: Article 300A (Right to property), Article 21 (Right to life – includes right to reputation and privacy).
• Objective: Ensure justice and compensation, not punishment.
