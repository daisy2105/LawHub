FAMILY LAW – KEY POINTS

• Regulates family-related issues: marriage, divorce, adoption, maintenance, succession.
• Different personal laws govern different communities (Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Parsi laws).
• Uniform Civil Code – Article 44 (Directive Principle).
• Protects rights of women and children within families.
• Key Acts: Hindu Marriage Act, 1955; Special Marriage Act, 1954; Guardians and Wards Act, 1890.
• Constitutional link: Article 15(3) (protection for women and children), Article 21 (right to dignity).
• Promotes family harmony, equality, and justice in personal matters.
