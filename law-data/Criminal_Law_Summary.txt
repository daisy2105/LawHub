CRIMINAL LAW – KEY POINTS

• Criminal law defines offences and prescribes punishments.
• Main objective: Maintain law, order, and protect society from crimes.
• Key codes: Indian Penal Code (IPC), Code of Criminal Procedure (CrPC), Indian Evidence Act.
• Crime is a public wrong – prosecuted by the State.
• Fundamental rights relevant: Article 20 (Protection in conviction), Article 21 (Right to life), Article 22 (Protection from arrest).
• Presumption of innocence – accused is innocent until proven guilty.
• Punishments include imprisonment, fine, probation, or death penalty (in rarest cases).
